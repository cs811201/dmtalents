MBP APIs

MBP provides built-in methods to access models and model parameters in MBP. There could be multiple models loaded in MBP at the same time; however, there is only one model called the "Main Model" at a given time, the model that's selected in the model panel currently.
  • getModels().getMainModel()
  • getModels().getModelCount()
  • getModels().getModel(int index)
  • toBinningMode()
  • toBinningPointMode()
  • getLocalModelManager().createTable(String outputNames)
  • getDataNames(String type)
  • getParam(String name)
  • getParams(String names)
  • getOptParam(String name)
  • getOptParams(String names)
  • getGUI
  • getSimulator
