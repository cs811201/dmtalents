About this Site

This site aims to provide ADDITIONAL application notes, best practice, and FAQs, etc material for Keysight end-to-end Device Modeling solution, including:
  • IC-CAP
  • Model Builder Program(MBP)
  • WaferPro | WaferPro Express
  • Molidel Quality Assurance(MQA)
  • Advanced Low Freq Noise Analyzer(A-LFNA)
Lots of items/functions explanations can be found in the software's user manual and will not be duplicated here.

All example files used in this website are from or created based on these products's example/demo folder.

All the materials provided in this website are solely from the good will for helping certificated/licensed Keysight users with their Device Modeling software application and usage; however, we are NOT responsible for any sort of work loss or failure from using them. Regular Save and Back-up your work are highly recommended.

Please still use Keysight formal channels for reporting bugs or new enhancements requests.
However, you are welcome to discuss the usage of any of the Keysight Device Modeling software with us.

Thank you for choosing Keysight!
