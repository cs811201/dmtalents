Corner Only

MQA FAQ

By creating a CornerQA project, we get the curves across all the selected corners, as shown below.



By default, we got the Corner Relation plots together with all the individual Corner plots, as highlighted in blue mask below:



Sometimes, these individual Corner's plots seem unnecessary, and users may want to get rid of them.

To do so, we just need to turn on the Option called "CornerOnly" when creating the CornerQA project, as shown below:


And, after deleting the old results and re-run the project, we get the result with "Corner Relation" only.
