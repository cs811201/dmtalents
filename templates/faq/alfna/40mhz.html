40MHz Limitation

ALFNA FAQ

ALFNA’s High Freq Voltage LNA can measure 1/f noise up to 40MHz; however, this capability may be limited by device’s roll-off frequency. Below is the illustration of the small signal circuitry that highlights the parasitics which play a role in determining the roll-off frequency.
