MBP Demo

SRAM Modeling Intro and Live Demo

This video begins with a 5 minutes introduction where we discuss the concept of SRAM cell modeling, the major challenges of the SRAM cell modeling process are highlighted and addressed, such as:
  • be capable of handling the SRAM cell macro model that consists of multiple core NMOS and PMOS devices
  • allow user to tune model parameters at both the SRAM and transistor levels.
  • view figures of merit at both SRAM and transistor levels
  • compare two SRAM macro models from foundry to foundry, or version to version.
Followed by another 5 minutes in-depth live demo that illustrates how easy MBP 2017 is to use and how it can save you time.
