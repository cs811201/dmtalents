How come the RMS became larger after Optimization

Well, this is a common error we made, the reason is because incidentally we had another region selected from another plot which may be hidden from current view.

A good habit we recommend to everyone, is, before every manual optimization, please use the Clear all regions button to clear all possible existing selection.
