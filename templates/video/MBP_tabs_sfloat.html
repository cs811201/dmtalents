Make MBP tabs/panels afloat

This is fairly easy, just double click on the tab of each panel, it will pop up, and we could resize it, or move it to another monitor to get a better view.

Almost all panel are floatable, such as Plot Browser, Device Navigator, Parameter table, IMV panel, etc.

To put a panel back if simple, too, just click the cross , it then goes back to the original place
