Spice Interpretation of S-Parameters

CONVERTING VERIFIED S-PARAMETERS TO PI or TEE SCHEMATICS

by Franz Sischka, www.SisConsult.de
In the previous chapter, it was shown that a raw Spice schematic can be developed directly from verified S-Parameter measurements. But such a quick Spice schematic is quite rough, and mainly valid for the lower part of the applied frequency range. For the development of a detailed Spice schematic, valid for the whole frequency range, the de-embedded and verified S-Parameters are converted to Y- or Z-Parameters. This enables the interpretation of the measurement with respect to a TEE or a PI schematic.
